Herein lies accounts of the major people of the Nevaria whether it be for political, cultural, or historical importance:

Pontius, The Golden King

The king of the central most island mapped on the Nevarian atlases. Many regard him to be the king of all of Nevaria.
He was made king after he sealed the Lich King Lodrin in an a tomb of eternal rest during The War of the Lich King.
Pontius is well respected by most all of the inhabitants of Nevaria for his great heroism and benevolence yet there
are still those who revile him.
