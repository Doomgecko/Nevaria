The Nevarian Pantheon

The Nevarian Pantheon is vast, encompassing a great number of cultures and religions. Some of the deities are Fey or Void beings from alternate planes of existence. Others are powerful angels and other celestial being or even ancient beings of great magic power. Below is a list encompassing many of the major deities:

Judicus, God of Justice

Momoah, God of Nature

Naudric, The Profaned God of the Black Gospel

Satantria, Goddess of Death

Gallius, God of the Forge

Sumetra, Queen of the Angels

Gradius, God of the Sea

Sathe, Flame of Hatred

Molrothaladach, God of Chaos

Azeroth, God of War

Molmmora, The World Eater

Mograt, God-King of Exiles

Micheal, The Grim Blade

Dagora, The Dream Eater

Malakanth, Ruler of the Underworld

Sulathra, God-Queen of the Frost Elves

Diamede, God of Wine and Revels

Saturnis, The Star God
