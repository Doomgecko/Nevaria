Herein lies accounts of the major people of Nevaria whether it be for political, cultural, or historical importance:

Pontius, The Golden King

The king of the central most island mapped on the Nevarian atlases. Many regard him to be the king of all of Nevaria.
He was made king after he sealed the Lich King Lodrin in an a tomb of eternal rest during The War of the Lich King.
Pontius is well respected by most all of the inhabitants of Nevaria for his great heroism and benevolence yet there
are still those who revile him.

Azer Malfurion

A renowned archmage, known for his famous book "The Inner Workings of Mana" which is widely regarded as the go to guide for magic. He developed many spells in his time researching the properties of mana, including the famous Magic Missile. He is most famous though for his magical library known as the Grand Arcanum. Inside the Grand Arcanum are thousands and thousands of books and scrolls concerning all different types of magic.
